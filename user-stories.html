User Stories

Homeowner Stories

As a homeowner, I would like to:

  • Properly sign in
  • Allow LivelyShelfs to access my location
  • Create a LivelyShelfs account
  • Let my Shelf Friends know that I am willing to share a specific food item
  • Share food items from my inventory with Shelf Friends
  • Add or remove Shelf Friends to share with
  • properly manage the food in the house inventory
  • Track the expiration status of food in my household
  • Receive incentives based on my LivelyShelfs usage
  • View the amount of food items I currently have in my inventory
  • Receive statistics of shared food within my Shelf Friends community

Shopper Stories

As a shopper, I would like to:

  • Receive notifications before purchasing food items based on inventory analytics
  • View data that charts my food usage into a visually pleasing display
  • Receive a suggested recipe for a selected item/items
